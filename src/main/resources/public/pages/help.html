mobile cirta social is a social application fully integrated with facebook products like login using facebook credentials and web hook functionalities
every operation made on mobile cirta social should be visible to all Facebook group members that have already subscribe to cirta-social
opening a session to someone without providing his Facebook account cannot be really useful since he wouldn’t take full advantage of what cirta-social offer.

Cirta-social is offering customizable interaction which suite every group on Facebook.
a group can share a custom content like for example documents (books or pages), those documents are domain specific (medicine, Mathematics …).
later on, every interaction is saved and propagated to all members.
